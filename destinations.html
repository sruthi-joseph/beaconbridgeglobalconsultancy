Study in Armenia

Armenia offers an excellent, affordable education system, specifically renowned for its medical universities. Students benefit from a rich cultural history and a welcoming environment.

  • WHO and NMC recognized medical programs
  • Low cost of living and tuition fees
  • English-medium instruction
  • Safe and student-friendly environment

Study in Kazakhstan

A rapidly developing educational hub in Central Asia, Kazakhstan boasts state-of-the-art medical and engineering universities with a multicultural student base.

  • Globally recognized universities
  • High-quality infrastructure and labs
  • Affordable tuition and living costs
  • Diverse and vibrant student life

Study in Georgia

Georgia is a prime European destination known for extremely safe cities and high-standard medical education that aligns with European guidelines.

  • 100% English-medium medical programs
  • No IELTS/TOEFL required for admission
  • Extremely low crime rate; very safe
  • Gateway to European opportunities

Study in Poland

Poland offers a proud academic tradition spanning centuries. Located in the heart of Europe, it provides world-class education at a fraction of the cost of Western Europe.

  • High-quality European education standards
  • Programs in engineering, business, and medicine
  • Access to the Schengen area
  • Booming economy with job opportunities

Study in Kyrgyzstan

Kyrgyzstan presents one of the most budget-friendly options globally for pursuing an MBBS degree, backed by institutions recognized by WHO and the NMC.

  • Highly affordable tuition fees
  • Practical, clinical-focused medical training
  • Simple and straightforward admission
  • Comfortable climate and living conditions

Study in Sweden

Study in Germany

As the economic powerhouse of Europe, Germany is renowned globally for its engineering, technology programs, and virtually free public university education.

  • No or very low tuition fees at public universities
  • Excellent post-study work regulations
  • Strong industry links for internships
  • World-class research facilities

Study in Malta

Study in the United Kingdom

The UK is home to some of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the world, offering unmatched academic rigor, shorter degree lengths, and global networking.

  • 1-year Masters and 3-year Bachelors programs
  • 2-year post-study work visa (Graduate Route)
  • Diverse, multicultural society
  • Unrivalled academic heritage and research

Study in Canada

Consistently ranked as one of the best places to live, Canada provides a world-class education with clear, welcoming pathways to long-term residency and careers.

  • High quality of life and spectacular landscapes
  • Up to 3-year Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP)
  • Co-op programs combining study and paid work
  • Extremely safe and inclusive society

Study in Australia

  • Generous post-study work rights

  • Stunning natural environment and beaches
